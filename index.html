About Me

Hello World! Just kidding. I'm about as tired seeing that phrase as I am writing it. Seriously though welcome to my online portfolio. If you can't already tell my personality is about as colorful as this site. I'm from Houston, Texas which is where I currently live. I have 3 children who take up a lot of my time but when I'm not laying the Smack Down on them I enjoy going to art shows, cooking or just plain nerding out on documentaries. I love to learn new things!

Right now I am working in the oil and gas industry as a mechanical designer creating downhole tools but I am looking to make a career change into web development. I love working with people on projects and I'm not afraid of a challenge. I have gone through numerous online tutorials and online courses including Udacity's Grow With Google Front End Web Development program. Two of my favorite developers that I follow and have taken online courses from are Wes Bos and Brad Traversy.
